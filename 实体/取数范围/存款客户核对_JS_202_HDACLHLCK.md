# 存款客户核对_JS_202_HDACLHLCK — 取数范围

> 来源程序: `bsp_sp_js_202_hdaclhlck.prc`

## 数据源表（FROM）

- L_PUBL_ORG_BRA_TMP AS OB
- SMTMODS.L_ACCT_DEPOSIT AS T1
- SMTMODS.L_CUST_P AS P

## 关联条件（JOIN）

- INNER JOIN SMTMODS.L_ACCT_DEPOSIT AS T1 ON T.ACCT_NUM = T1.ACCT_NUM AND T1.DATA_DATE = IS_DATE
- INNER JOIN SMTMODS.L_CUST_P AS P ON T.CUST_ID = P.CUST_ID AND P.DATA_DATE = IS_DATE
- INNER JOIN L_PUBL_ORG_BRA_TMP AS OB ON OB.ORG_NUM = T.ORG_NUM AND OB.DATA_DATE = IS_DATE

## 筛选条件（WHERE）

1. `T.DATA_DATE = IS_DATE`
2. `T.MEDIUM_STAT = 'A'`
3. `SUBSTRING(T.TYPE_ID, 1, 8) IN ('62313113', '62313118')`
4. `T1.ACCT_BALANCE <> 0`
